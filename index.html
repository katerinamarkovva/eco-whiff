HOW TO USE

How Long do they last?

Our Air Purifier / Shoe Deodorizers are reusable for up two years! From a cost perspective, our deodorizers will save money and help remove Foot odor from your shoes for longer.

How do you Recharge?

To ensure long life in your deodorizers, once a month you need to hang your Air Purifiers in direct Sunlight to absorb the UV rays. This will recharge the pores of the activated bamboo charcoal, making it ready to absorb again.

And when their Finished?

Between 1 to 2 years your shoe deodorizer will slow down the amount they absorb, at this point you can simply cut open the bag and sprinkle the Activated bamboo charcoal into the soil.
